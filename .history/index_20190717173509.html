Introduction

The Salt Lake County vehicle emissions testing program suffers from uncontrolled transactional cost burdens for motorists and is not capable of tracking noncompliant vehicles. How can we leverage Salt Lake County test pricing and vehicle data to reduce cost inefficiencies for motorists and enable tracking capabilities for local government?
